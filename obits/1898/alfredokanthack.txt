Alfredo Kanthack



He died of cancer on 21 December 1898 aged 35. After his funeral in Kings College Chapel, he was buried at Histon Road Cemetery, Cambridge.

After his death his wife donated funds in his memory to establish a library in the Pathology Department which still bears his name, and a bronze memorial plaque was placed at the entrance in Tennis Court Road.

A sum of money was collected after his death to establish  The Kanthack Memorial Library  in the Pathological Institute of St Bartholomew's Hospital.

The Kanthack Medal has been awarded at Liverpool since 1900 for experimental pathology and microbiology, reflecting the importance of his work there.
