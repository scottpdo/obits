Peter St. Albans



He died at the home for his father in law, Mr William Ryan at Geelong on 23 July 1898. Peter Bowden aka St. Albans was survived by his wife, Asastasia.   He was buried at The Eastern Cemetery, Geelong on 25 July 1898. The funeral was watched by hundreds of locals and was possibly the biggest funeral in the history of Geelong at the time.
