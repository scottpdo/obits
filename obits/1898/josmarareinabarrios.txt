José María Reina Barrios



  Palace of La Reforma. Built by Reyna Barrios between 1892 and 1898.  it was destroyed by the 1917 Guatemala earthquake.


José María Reina was assassinated on 8 February 1898 in  Ciudad de Guatemala, by Edgar Zollinger, a British citizen of Swiss stock, shortly after his reelection. Reina Barrios, who was interred in the Metropolitan Cathedral, was succeeded as President by Manuel Estrada Cabrera.
